About Me

Hi! My name is Karla Alejandro, I’m a multidisciplinary designer and visual artist with a passion for creativity in all its forms.

I hold a Bachelor’s degree in Industrial Design from Universidad La Salle Saltillo Mexico (2018–2022), and I’m currently completing a Master’s degree in Business and Administration at Universidad Americana del Noreste Mexico, along with an Associate degree in Digital Art & Design at Dallas College.

Born and raised in Mexico, I grew up moving often due to my mother’s career—an experience that deeply shaped my adaptability and artistic perspective. My work is grounded in fine arts, especially painting and drawing, but I thrive in exploring new mediums and combining traditional techniques with modern technology. Whether I’m developing brand identities, crafting immersive AR experiences, or creating expressive illustrations, I approach every project with originality, curiosity, and a love for detail.

At 26, I see myself as a creative jack-of-all-trades—constantly learning, evolving, and pushing the boundaries of what art and design can be.
